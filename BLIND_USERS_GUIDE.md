## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### **"Voice navigation not working"**

**Check:**
1. Microphone permission granted?
2. Using Chrome or Edge browser?
3. Microphone working in other apps?
4. Volume turned up?

**Try:**
1. Refresh the page (F5)
2. Try the top bar button instead
3. Check browser console (F12)

### **"Can't find the voice button"**

**Solution:**
1. Press Tab repeatedly
2. Listen for "Voice Nav button"
3. It's in the top bar (early in tab order)
4. Press Enter when you find it

### **"Commands not recognized"**

**Try:**
1. Speak louder
2. Say just the keyword ("Lessons")
3. Wait a moment after speaking
4. Check microphone is not muted
