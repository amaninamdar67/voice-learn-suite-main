## Benefits

✅ **Forgiving** - Misspellings don't break navigation
✅ **Natural** - Say it how you think it
✅ **Fast** - Use short forms to save time
✅ **Flexible** - Multiple ways to say the same thing
✅ **Accessible** - Works even with pronunciation variations
✅ **Smart** - Recognizes common mistakes

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use Short Forms** - "Study" instead of "Study Materials"
2. **Don't Worry About Spelling** - "Recourses" works just like "Resources"
3. **Singular or Plural** - Both work equally well
4. **Spacing Doesn't Matter** - "Leaderboard" = "Leader board"
5. **Just Say Key Words** - "Quiz" is enough, no need for "Take Quiz"
