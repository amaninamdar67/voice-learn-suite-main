### 1. Enable Voice Navigation

### 3. Say Commands

#### Navigate
- "go to dashboard"
- "go to settings"
- "go to leaderboard"
- "go back"

#### Open Content
- "open video 1" (or "one", "two", "three")
- "open quiz 2"
- "open assignment 3"
- "open item 1"

#### Read Pages
- "read page" - starts reading
- "pause" - pause reading
- "continue" - resume
- "next page" - skip ahead
- "previous page" - go back
- "stop" - stop completely

#### Scroll & Navigate
- "scroll up"
- "scroll down"
- "next"
- "previous"
- "close"

#### Search
- "search for mathematics"

#### Turn Off
- "voice navigation off" - enters wake-word mode
- "voice navigation on" - re-enables from wake-word mode
