## âœ… How to Use

### Create a Link
1. Go to **"Un-Linked Students"**
2. Click **"Create Link"**
3. Student moves to **"Linked Students"**

### Link All 3 at Once
1. Go to **"Linked Students"**
2. Click **"Link"**
3. Form opens
4. Select:
   - Parent
   - Relationship
   - Mentor
5. Click **"Link All"**

### View Links
1. Go to **"Link Info"**
2. See all linked students with data

### Edit Links
1. Go to **"Link Info"**
2. Click **"Edit"** (pencil icon)
3. Form opens
4. Modify as needed
5. Click **"Link All"**

### Delete Links
1. Go to **"Link Info"**
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. Click **"Delete"**
4. Confirm
5. All links deleted
